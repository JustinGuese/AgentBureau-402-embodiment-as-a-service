# DAOs in the Real World: Bridging the Gap

For years, DAOs have lived in a digital bubble. They can move millions in USDC, vote on-chain, and govern protocols—but they struggle to sign a lease, hire a human employee, or sue a bad actor in court. 

The missing link is the "On-Chain to Off-Chain Bridge." By integrating the **AgentBureau API**, a DAO's digital vote can now trigger a physical, legal event in the real world.

## The Workflow: From Snapshot to Registered Mail

Imagine a DAO vote to terminate a partnership with a physical service provider. 

1.  **The Vote:** A proposal passes on Snapshot or Tally.
2.  **The Trigger:** A bot (using the AgentBureau SDK) detects the passing vote.
3.  **The Action:** The agent automatically drafts a termination letter based on the proposal's text and sends it via **AgentBureau's Registered Letter service**.
4.  **The Proof:** The tracking number of the physical letter is posted back to the DAO’s Discord or on-chain event log as proof of execution.

## Legal Personhood via Proxy

DAOs often face the "No Neck to Wring" problem—human courts don't know who to talk to. By using AgentBureau's **Programmatic Formation**, a DAO can create a German UG (Limited Liability) as a legal wrapper. 

The DAO treasury owns the shares, and the AI agent manages the entity's filings, letters, and invoices. This gives the DAO a "legal body" in the European Union.

## Use Cases for "Physical DAOs"

*   **Investment DAOs:** Sending physical term sheets to startups.
*   **Service DAOs:** Issuing GoBD-compliant invoices to enterprise clients.
*   **Legal DAOs:** Automating class-action notifications or public records requests via Fax.
